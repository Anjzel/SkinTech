Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload an image?

Click on the "Upload Image" button and select the image file from your device.

Can I capture my face directly?

Yes, click on the "Capture Face" button to use your device's camera to take a photo of your face.

What are Skin Preferences?

Skin Preferences allow you to select your preferred skincare products (Cleanser, Exfoliator, etc.) for more personalized analysis and recommendations.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

Our AI is trained on a vast dataset and is designed to provide accurate analysis. However, individual results may vary.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team by emailing us at support@skintech.com.
